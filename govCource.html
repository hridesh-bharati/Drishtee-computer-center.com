DOEACC Certificate Courses on Computer Concepts (CCC)

DOEACC’s courses have been designed to make Computer Applications and IT literacy more widespread in India. The first of all course levels in DOEACC is CCC or Course on Computer Concept.

‘O’ Level Course

DOEACC ‘O’ Level course has been recognized by the Ministry of Human Resources Development as being similar to a Foundation Level Course in Computer Applications. The one year course covers programming languages and other IT basics. There are also practical papers and a project to be covered as part of the syllabus and in order to earn the ‘O’ level.
